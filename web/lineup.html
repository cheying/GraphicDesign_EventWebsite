Featured Speaker

Koonz





This lovely free spirit has unknown origins, no documented identity, and no fingerprints at that. She frolics the streets unmasked, prowling the streets in a stealthy manner. This year she will grace our convention's presence to discuss key breaking-and-entering skills, and how to contort your body in to tight places.

Performers/Speakers

DJ Mikey

DJ Mikey hails from the streets of Chicago. He rose to the top of the charts in the blink of an eye, and now he is a fond supporter of vigilantism. His beats are all home made at his penthouse suite. He mixes both instrumentals and incorporates his own rap vocals on his tracks. He will be proudly DJing at our After Dark Gala.

Cosplay Bandit AKA "COS"

COS has a style enviable by professional cosplayers worldwide. Always in full makeup, wig/extensions, and costume accessories she is as KAWAII as it gets! She has a big heart and is known for aiding those with mental and physical disabilities. She will be holding a meditation session, and judging the Costume Contest.

The LOVOXO

The LOVOXO will make his first appearance at V-Con this year! His crime-fighting abilities are as mysterious as his origins and perogative. He will lead our open-discussion about current crimes, high priority criminals, and unconventional means of subduing perpetrators.

Yeezy

Yeezy has invited himself as a motivational speaker this year. He will not tolerate being referred to by his "birth name" so remember to call him Yeezy, just Yeezy. He will also being selling his merchandise at the V Market this year so please humor him as he likes to get himself involved in EVERYTHING... even vigilante conventions...
